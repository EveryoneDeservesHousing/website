Support EDH

Help make stable housing possible.

Everyone Deserves Housing exists to ensure people experiencing homelessness have access to safe, dignified shelter and the support they need to rebuild their lives.

Make a contribution

Financial contributions allow EDH to respond flexibly to urgent needs, from basic operations to client services and program startup costs.

  • Provide safe, indoor shelter
  • Support people as they stabilize and move toward permanent housing
  • Build sustainable, evidence-based services grounded in dignity and respect
Donate

Support shelter residents

You can directly support individuals living in shelter by purchasing commonly needed items through the EDH wish list.

  • Bedding and personal care items
  • Clothing and weather-appropriate gear
  • Household and comfort items for shared living spaces
