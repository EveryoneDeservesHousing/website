## Slide 5

**Intentional Throughput**

We will function as a direct bridge to housing, recognizing that people experiencing street-level homelessness require different levels of intervention to stabilize.

For individuals who can quickly regain housing stability, services will support access to scattered-site rapid rehousing, delivered in close coordination with shelter and outreach teams to ensure continuity, engagement, and problem-solving support during the transition.

For individuals with higher acuity needs who require long-term housing and services, the nonprofit will facilitate access to permanent supportive housing through the region's coordinated entry system.
